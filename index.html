Common Questions

Things You Might Be Wondering

Where are you based, and do you work outside Brisbane?

We're based in Brisbane, but we take on projects across South East Queensland, the Sunshine Coast, Gold Coast, and beyond — including interstate and international work. Current projects span from Byron Bay to California.

How much does a project typically cost?

Every project is different. Investment ranges and timelines are discussed during your Introductory Design Call, and confirmed after we've walked through your brief together in a Vision Session. We'll help you identify the right service tier for your scope.

Do I need to have plans or an architect already?

Not at all. Our Signature service covers everything from architecture and town planning through to styling. If you already have an architect or builder, our Curated and Atelier tiers pick up where they leave off.

How long does a project take?

It depends on scale and complexity. Thoughtfully designed homes naturally unfold over time — some projects move through construction across many months, and larger projects over several years. We'll give you a clear timeline early on.

Will I be working directly with Natalie?

Yes. Natalie personally leads every project. She's supported by a small, hand-picked team of designers, architects, and collaborators — but she remains your primary creative director and point of contact.

What types of projects do you take on?

New builds, knock-down rebuilds, renovations, refurbishments, interior design, and furnishing and styling. We work across residential projects of all scales — from apartment interiors to full architectural builds.
